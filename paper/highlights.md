# Highlights

- Forest cover declined 43.9% (2,599,275 to 1,457,337 ha) in Colombia's Magdalena Medio (2013-2024).
- Post-peace agreement deforestation accelerated, with 986,100 ha of forest-to-pasture conversion.
- Carbon losses totaled 190 Mt C (33.5% decline), equivalent to approximately 697 Mt CO2.
- Geographically weighted regression (R2=0.609) revealed spatially varying deforestation drivers.
- Conservation scenario projects 72.5% forest recovery by 2040 versus 60.9% under business-as-usual.
